Regular Ol' Broccoli

Broccoli is broccoli. It's a plant and, as such, should generally be shunned around the holidays. No one wants to eat something green that doesn't taste like cookies or mints. That said, your mother-in-law is visiting, and she's going to judge you if you don't put something fibrous and flavorless into your mouth.

You will have to eat this to keep the peace. But don't worry. We'll do it up right so that it mostly tastes like butter and salt.

Ingredients

  • 16 ounce bag frozen broccoli
  • 4 T butter
  • 1 T bacon fat
  • 1 T salt
  • more salt to taste

Steps

  1. Boil some water.
  2. Cook the broccoli in it for five minutes.
  3. Drain broccoli in a colander and put in a serving bowl.
  4. While still hot, melt butter and bacon fat onto broccoli and mix.
  5. That's it. Really. Add more salt if you need to, but just get it down so that you can move on to dessert with a clear conscience.
