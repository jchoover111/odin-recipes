Regular Ol' Broccoli

Broccoli is broccoli. It's a plant and, as such, should generally be shunned around the holidays. No one wants to eat something green that doesn't taste like cookies or mints. That said, your mother-in-law is visiting, and she's going to harangue you until you put something fibrous and flavorless into your mouth.

You will have to eat this to keep the peace. But don't worry. We'll do it up right so that it mostly tastes like butter and salt.
